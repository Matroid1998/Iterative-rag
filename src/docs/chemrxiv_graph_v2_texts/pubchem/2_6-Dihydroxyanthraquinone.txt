Name: 2,6-Dihydroxyanthraquinone
Text:
Anthraflavic acid is a dihydroxyanthraquinone that is anthracene substituted by hydroxy groups at C-3 and C-7 and oxo groups at C-9 and C-10. It has a role as an antimutagen and a plant metabolite. It derives from a hydride of an anthracene.
2,6-Dihydroxyanthraquinone has been reported in Senna obtusifolia and Rubia tinctorum with data available.
