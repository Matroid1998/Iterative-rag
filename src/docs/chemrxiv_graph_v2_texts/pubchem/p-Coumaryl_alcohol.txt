Name: p-Coumaryl alcohol
Text:
Trans-p-coumaryl alcohol is 4-Hydroxycinnamyl alcohol with E-configuration of the propenyl double bond. It is one of the main monolignols. It has a role as a monolignol. It is a phenylpropanoid, a member of phenols and a 4-hydroxycinnamyl alcohol. It is functionally related to an (E)-cinnamyl alcohol.
p-Coumaryl alcohol has been reported in Solanum tuberosum, Alpinia officinarum, and other organisms with data available.
