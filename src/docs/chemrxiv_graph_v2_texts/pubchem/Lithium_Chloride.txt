Name: Lithium Chloride
Text:
Lithium chloride appears as colorless crystals or powder. Low toxicity.
Lithium chloride is a metal chloride salt with a Li(+) counterion. It has a role as an antimanic drug and a geroprotector. It is an inorganic chloride and a lithium salt.
A salt of lithium that has been used experimentally as an immunomodulator.
