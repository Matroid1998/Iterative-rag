Text:
Propionamide is a monocarboxylic acid amide obtained by the formal condensation of propionic acid with ammonia. It is a monocarboxylic acid amide and a primary fatty amide. It is functionally related to a propionic acid.
Propionamide has been reported in Bongardia chrysogonum with data available.
