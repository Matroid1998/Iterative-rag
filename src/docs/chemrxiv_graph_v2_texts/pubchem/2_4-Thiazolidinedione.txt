Name: 2,4-Thiazolidinedione
Text:
1,3-thiazolidine-2,4-dione is a thiazolidenedione carrying oxo substituents at positions 2 and 4. It derives from a hydride of a 1,3-thiazolidine.
Thiazolidinedione has been investigated for the treatment of Type 2 Diabetes, Diabetes Mellitus, and DIABETES MELLITUS, TYPE 2.
THIAZOLIDINEDIONE is a small molecule drug with a maximum clinical trial phase of III (across all indications) and has 5 investigational indications.
