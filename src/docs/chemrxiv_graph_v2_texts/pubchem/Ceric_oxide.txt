Name: Ceric oxide
Text:
Ceric oxide is a metal oxide with formula CeO2. It is used for polishing glass, in coatings for infra-red filters to prevent reflection, and as an oxidant and catalyst in organic synthesis. It is a cerium molecular entity and a metal oxide.
