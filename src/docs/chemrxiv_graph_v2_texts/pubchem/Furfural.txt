Furfural is an aldehyde that is furan with the hydrogen at position 2 substituted by a formyl group. It has a role as a Maillard reaction product and a metabolite. It is a member of furans and an aldehyde. It is functionally related to a furan.
Furfural has been reported in Francisella tularensis, Angelica gigas, and other organisms with data available.
