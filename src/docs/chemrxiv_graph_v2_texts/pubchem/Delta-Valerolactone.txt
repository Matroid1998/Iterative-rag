Name: Delta-Valerolactone
Text:
5-valerolactone is the simplest member of the class of delta-lactone that is tetrahydro-2H-pyran substituted by an oxo group at position 2. It derives from a hydride of an oxane.
delta-Valerolactone has been reported in Clerodendrum mandarinorum and Aspalathus linearis with data available.
