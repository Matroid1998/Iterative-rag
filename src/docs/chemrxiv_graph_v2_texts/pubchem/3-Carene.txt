Name: 3-Carene
Text:
Carene is a colorless liquid with a sweet, turpentine-like odor. Floats on water. (USCG, 1999)
Car-3-ene is a monoterpene. It derives from a hydride of a carane.
3-Carene has been reported in Artemisia thuscula, Cymbopogon martinii, and other organisms with data available.
See also: Cannabis sativa subsp. indica top (part of); (+)-3-Carene (annotation moved to).
