Name: Phenethylamine
Text:
2-phenylethylamine is a phenylethylamine having the phenyl substituent at the 2-position. It has a role as a human metabolite, an Escherichia coli metabolite and a mouse metabolite. It is a phenylethylamine, an aralkylamine and an alkaloid. It is a conjugate base of a 2-phenylethanaminium.
Phenylethylamine is a metabolite found in or produced by Escherichia coli (strain K12, MG1655).
Phenethylamine has been reported in Senegalia berlandieri, Sedum lydium, and other organisms with data available.
Phenylethylamine is a metabolite found in or produced by Saccharomyces cerevisiae.
