Name: Triphenylphosphine
Text:
Triphenyl phosphine appears as white crystals. (NTP, 1992)
Triphenylphosphine is a member of the class of tertiary phosphines that is phosphane in which the three hydrogens are replaced by phenyl groups. It has a role as a reducing agent and a NMR chemical shift reference compound. It is a member of benzenes and a tertiary phosphine.
