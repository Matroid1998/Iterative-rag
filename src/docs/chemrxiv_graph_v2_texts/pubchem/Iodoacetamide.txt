Name: Iodoacetamide
Text:
Iodoacetamide is a sulfhydryl alkylating reagent that forms covalent bonds with the thiol group of cysteines so the protein or peptide no longer forms disulfide bonds.
An alkylating sulfhydryl reagent. Its actions are similar to those of iodoacetate.
