Name: Ammonium Hydroxide
Text:
Ammonium hydroxide appears as a colorless aqueous solution. Concentration of ammonia ranges up to approximately 30%. Ammonia vapors (which arise from the solution) irritate the eyes.
Ammonium hydroxide is a solution of ammonia in water. It has a role as a food acidity regulator.
The hydroxy salt of ammonium ion. It is formed when AMMONIA reacts with water molecules in solution.
See also: Ammonia (subclass of).
