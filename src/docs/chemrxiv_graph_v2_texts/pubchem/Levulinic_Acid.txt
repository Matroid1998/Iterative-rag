Name: Levulinic Acid
Text:
4-oxopentanoic acid is an oxopentanoic acid with the oxo group in the 4-position. It has a role as a plant metabolite. It is a straight-chain saturated fatty acid and an oxopentanoic acid. It is a conjugate acid of a 4-oxopentanoate.
Levulinic acid is a metabolite found in or produced by Escherichia coli (strain K12, MG1655).
Levulinic acid has been reported in Dendronephthya hemprichi with data available.
LEVULINIC ACID is a small molecule drug with a maximum clinical trial phase of III.
