Name: 4-Tolyl isocyanate
Text:
4-tolyl isocyanate is an isocyanate comprising a benzene core with isocyanato and methyl substituents para to each other. It has a role as a hapten and an allergen. It is a member of isocyanates and a member of toluenes.
