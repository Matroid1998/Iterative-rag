Name: Boron phosphide
Text:
Boron phosphide is a phosphide of boron. It is a semiconductor. Metal phosphides are hydrolysed to phosphine upon contact with water or stomach acid. Phosphine is a colorless, flammable, explosive, and toxic gas. (L980, L983)
