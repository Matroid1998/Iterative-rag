Name: 2'-Hydroxyacetophenone
Text:
2-acetylphenol is a monohydroxyacetophenone that is acetophenone in which one of the hydrogens ortho to the acetyl group has been replaced by a hydroxy group. It has a role as a flavouring agent. It is a monohydroxyacetophenone and a member of phenols.
2'-Hydroxyacetophenone has been reported in Aronia melanocarpa, Paeonia lactiflora, and other organisms with data available.
