Pyrazine is a diazine that is benzene in which the carbon atoms at positions 1 and 4 have been replaced by nitrogen atoms. It has a role as a Daphnia magna metabolite. It is a member of pyrazines and a diazine.
Pyrazine has been reported in Nicotiana tabacum, Coffea arabica, and Daphnia magna with data available.
PYRAZINE is a small molecule drug with a maximum clinical trial phase of III and has 1 investigational indication.
