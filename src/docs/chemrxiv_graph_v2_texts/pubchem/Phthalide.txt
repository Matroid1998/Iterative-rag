Text:
2-benzofuran-1(3H)-one is a gamma-lactone that is 1,3-dihydro-2-benzofuran in which the hydrogens at position 1 are replaced by an oxo group. It is a gamma-lactone and a member of 2-benzofurans.
Phthalide has been reported in Ligusticum striatum, Angelica sinensis, and Ligusticum chuanxiong with data available.
