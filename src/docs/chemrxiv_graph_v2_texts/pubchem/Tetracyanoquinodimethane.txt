Name: Tetracyanoquinodimethane
Text:
Tetracyanoquinodimethane is a quinodimethane that is p-quinodimethane in which the methylidene hydrogens are replaced by cyano groups. It is an alicyclic compound, a nitrile and a quinodimethane. It is functionally related to a p-quinodimethane.
Tetracyanoquinodimethane is an organic compound of cyanide. It is an electron-acceptor that is used to prepare charge transfer salts, which are of interest in the area of molecular electronics. (L614)
