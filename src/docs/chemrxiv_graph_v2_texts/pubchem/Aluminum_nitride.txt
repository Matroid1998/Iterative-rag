Aluminium nitride is a nitride.
Aluminium nitride (AlN) is a nitride of aluminium. It is a semiconductor and can be used in light-emitting diodes. Aluminum is the most abundant metal in the earth's crust and is always found combined with other elements such as oxygen, silicon, and fluorine.  (L739, L740, T10)
