Name: Ammonium formate
Text:
Ammonium formate is a white solid with a weak odor of ammonia. Sinks and mixes slowly with water. (USCG, 1999)
Ammonium formate is the ammonium salt of formic acid. It has a role as a buffer. It is functionally related to a formic acid.
