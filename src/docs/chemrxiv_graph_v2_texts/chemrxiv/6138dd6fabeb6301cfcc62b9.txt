Description:
Materials with promising potentials in optoelectronics and photocatalysis.

Text:
Inorganic cesium lead halide perovskites have attracted intense research interest for their 
promising potentials in applications such as optoelectronics13 and photocatalysis.4 The advances 
in nanotechnology enable precise control of the size5, shape69, and chemical doping1015 of 
inorganic perovskite nanocrystals (PNCs), providing abundant approaches to tune the optical and 
electronic properties of this material. Surface chemistry is one of the most intriguing fields in the research of nanomaterials.
