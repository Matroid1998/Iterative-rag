Transition metal complexes have made a lasting impact in medicine with the platinum
based anticancer drugs constituting some of the most effective chemotherapeutic 
cancer regimens in the clinic today.6,7 Over the last decade, more and more studies 
have highlighted the promising antimicrobial properties of metal complexes.811 The 
recent progress in this field has been highlighted in several review articles.12,13 We 
have recently shown that metal complexes have superior hitrates against critical 
bacteria14 and fungi15 in vitro compared to purely organic molecules.
