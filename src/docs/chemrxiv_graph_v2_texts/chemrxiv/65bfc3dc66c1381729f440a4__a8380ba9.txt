Description:
A component that can be present in soft drinks.

Text:
Soft aerated beverages also known as carbonated drinks or soft drinks is a non
alcoholic, light flavoured, sweet, waterbased drink in which carbon dioxide is added to make 
them fizzy or bubbly. Various type of flavouring agents such as artificial or natural are used, 
which are colored and can also contain fruit pulp, fruit juice or caffeine. The sweetener used 
maybe fruit juice, sugar or sugar substitutes or fructose corn syrup. Based on different regions 
they have names such as soda pop, fizzy drink, coke, pop etc. Fruit punch, fruit drinks, iced tea, coffee with sugar, and sports drinks are examples of non
aerated soft beverages, which are beverages without carbon dioxide and sparkling flavour.
