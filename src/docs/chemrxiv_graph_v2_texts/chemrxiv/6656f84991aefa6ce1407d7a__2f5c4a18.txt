The chemical formula for 5fluorouracil.

Text:
5fluorouracil (Figure 1, C4H3FN2O2, IUPAC name 5fluoro1Hpyrimidine2,4dione, CAS ID 
51218, PubChem CID 33851) is an antimetabolite drug that is widely used in the treatment of 
various cancers, including colorectal cancer and breast cancer. Several modulation strategies 
have been developed to improve the therapeutic effectiveness and cytotoxicity of 5FU 
treatment.2,3 Although 5FU is considered to be one of the safest chemotherapeutic agents, its 
toxicity can cause severe side effects (including fever, fatigue, and nausea) and toxic effects 
(including leukopenia and anaemia).
