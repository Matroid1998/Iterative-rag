Description:
A natural product that binds to the FK506 binding protein 51 (FKBP51).

Text:
The FK506binding protein 51 (FKBP51, encoded by the FKBP 5 gene) is a cochaperone of the 
Hsp90 machinery and binds the natural products FK506 and Rapamycin.1 FKBP51 plays a 
prominent role in mammalian stress biology.
